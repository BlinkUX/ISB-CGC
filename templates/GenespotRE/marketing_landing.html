Bring your data stories to life

Avance is a powerful, web-based application that enables biologists to easily analyze and visualize large data sets in a collaborative environment.

A faster, more collaborative path from questions to answers.

Avance was created by the Institute for Systems Biology with support from the National Cancer Institute and the Life Sciences Discovery Fund. Our goal is to enable biologists to perform large scale integrative data analysis more efficiently, without requiring coding skills or the resources of a big informatics department

The easy to use, web-based interface promotes collaboration and helps researchers easily compare their own data to publically available research. All storage, transformation and manipulation of data happens in the cloud, freeing researchers to focus on analysis.

Avance is currently in a public beta phase, and the entire platform is available to all users free of charge.

Simple, Intuitive Interface

Easily upload your own data, select your variables, and interact with your research in a visual format. No coding required.

Multiple Statistical Tests & Visualization Styles

Run multiple statistical tests on your data a see it presented through different visualizations with just a few mouse clicks. Easily export compelling visual assets for presentation or publication.

Workbook + Worksheets Model

Create and save multiple analyses of a data set, compare different variables or groups as you build your data story.

Real-Time Comparisons

View and interact with your data side by side with publically available data or data from other researchers and collaborators.

Facilitate Collaboration

Enable collaborators near and far to share data and collaborate on analysis in real time.

Web-Based Infrastructure
