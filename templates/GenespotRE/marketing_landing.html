A faster, more collaborative path from questions to answers.

Avance was created by the Institute for Systems Biology with support from the National Cancer Institute and the Life Sciences Discovery Fund. Our goal is to enable biologists to perform large scale integrative data analysis more efficiently, without requiring coding skills or the resources of a big informatics department.

The easy to use, web-based interface promotes collaboration and helps researchers easily compare their own data to publically available research. All storage, transformation and manipulation of data happens in the cloud, freeing researchers to focus on analysis.

A Simple, Intuitive Way to Interact with Your Data

Upload your data, select your variables, and interact with your research through web-based visualizations. No coding required.

Multiple Statistical Tests & Visualization Styles

Avance will automatically run the most appropriate statistical tests based on the visualization—or visualizations—you select.

Workbook
+ Worksheets Model

Create and save multiple analyses of a data set, compare different variables or groups as you build your data story.

Real-Time Comparisons

View and interact with your data side by side with publically available data or data from other researchers and collaborators.

Facilitate Collaboration

Enable collaborators near and far to share data and collaborate on analysis in real time.

Web-Based Infrastructure

All storage, transformation and manipulation of data happens in the cloud, freeing researchers to focus on analysis.

  • 01

    UPLOAD YOUR DATA

    Avance accepts raw data in most standard formats, so you can begin performing analysis as soon as you get your data. (You can also interact with our library of publically available data sets.)

  • 02

    ADD VARIABLES AND COHORTS

    Slice and segment your data and easily pull in publically available data sets from our library. Different cohorts and variables are clearly segmented for easy, one-click comparisons.

  • 03

    ANALYZE AND VISUALIZE YOUR DATA

    Choose from our ever-growing library of statistical tests and corresponding visualizations.  The avance application can perform a variety of simple-to-complex quantitative analyses and provide an appropriate, interactive plot of the results.

  • 04

    SHARE AND COLLABORATE

    Easily share data stories right from the app. Invite team members or colleagues to view and interact with your data, upload their own, and collaborate on analyses. You can even export visualizations for presentation or publication.

  • 05

    POWERFUL ANALYSIS
    AND POWERFUL TEAMWORK

    Use the “Workbook/Worksheet” model to create multiple projects or working groups and easily facilitate side-by-side comparisons of different analyses and visualizations.
